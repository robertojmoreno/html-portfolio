My favorites Movies

The best movies according to Roberto

Life Is Beautiful

Directed by Roberto Benigni, is a touching film blending humor, tragedy, and romance. Set in Italy during World War II, it tells the story of Guido, a Jewish Italian bookshop owner. He uses his imagination and humor to shield his son from the horrors of a Nazi concentration camp. The film beautifully portrays the power of love and resilience in the face of brutality. Despite the grim setting, it maintains a light-hearted tone, emphasizing the importance of preserving innocence and joy even in the darkest times. It's a poignant reminder of the strength of the human spirit.

The Pursuit of Happyness

a film starring Will Smith. It's an inspiring story based on the real-life struggles of Chris Gardner. Smith plays Gardner, a struggling salesman who becomes homeless with his young son. Despite immense hardships, Gardner never gives up. He takes an unpaid internship in a competitive stockbroker training program while sheltering his son in homeless shelters. His perseverance and dedication eventually lead to a successful career as a stockbroker. The film highlights the themes of resilience, the bond between father and son, and the pursuit of the American dream against all odds. It's a powerful testament to the human spirit's resilience and the importance of hope.

Coco

"Coco" is a vibrant and heartfelt movie that delves into themes of family, dreams, and the importance of remembering loved ones. It follows Miguel, a young boy with a passion for music, which clashes with his family's ban on music. On the Day of the Dead, Miguel finds himself in the colorful Land of the Dead. There, he uncovers forgotten family secrets and the story of his great-great-grandfather, a legendary musician. The film beautifully weaves Mexican culture and traditions into its narrative, exploring how our connections with family shape our identity and how memories keep our loved ones alive in our hearts. It's a poignant tale of self-discovery, reconciliation, and the power of music.
